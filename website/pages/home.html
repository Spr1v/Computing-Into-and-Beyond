What is Computing?

Computing is the process of using technology and algorithms to process, store, and analyze information. It encompasses a wide range of activities, from simple calculations to complex problem-solving tasks, utilizing hardware like computers and smartphones, along with software applications. Modern computing drives advancements in fields like artificial intelligence, communication, healthcare, and more, enabling efficient data management and automation in everyday life.
