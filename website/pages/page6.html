Comparative Study on Different types of computers

"Diverse systems reflect the versatility of computers."
What is a computer?

It is an electronic device designed to process, store, and retrieve data. It performs a variety of tasks based on the instructions provided, making it a versatile tool for work, communication, entertainment, and more. Through commands, it can execute calculations, manage information, and interact with other devices or users
Supercomputer

- Used in the field of Science, Technology, Medicine, and more, this computer allows users to compute complex and heavy computations for their need. As the most powerful system and computer in known history, it has allowed breakthrough in certain fields that needs such a huge computing power.
Mainframe

- Used by large organizations to run applications that need high levels of processing power, storage, and most importantly reliability. Organizations need this as it is important in maintaining the company’s technological structure of handling transactions, data access, data analysis, and more
Mini Computer

- Used in businesses and personal use, making it favorable for handling jobs with multiple tasks as it had lesser cost and efficiency. It is also used within smaller departments that still need computational power for their day to day job, but not as intensive as a mainframe.
Server

- Used as a central hub that manages all the information that comes in and out which are accessed by multiple clients, as it is a huge database. This is commonly used by large organizations that need to store their data in a safe place, while allowing employees to review or write data within.
Workstation Computers

- Used by professionals in fields that require heavy computer simulations, such as video editing, rendering 3D objects, which is perfect for intensive applications like these. These computers are very work specific, which is why most animations and renders use this type of computer
Micro Computers

- Used by the many as a general purpose computer, it allows users to access entertainment, browsing, communicating, which does not require heavy processing power. This is perfect for everyone day to day life, as most of the applications today do not require heavy processing power.
