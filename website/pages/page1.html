Computer Science as a Discipline, Computing Disciplines and Majors

"Computer Science drives innovation across computing disciplines."
Computer Science
Concept
- revolves around the study of algorithms, programming, data structures, and computational theories to solve complex problems.

the backbone of innovation in artificial intelligence, machine learning, software engineering, and advanced data analytics.
Impact on Society

- Innovations in AI industry

- Logics to enable automation of day to day tasks

- Smarter systems to handle computations, alogithms, and etc

Computer Engineering
Concept
- By utilizing science, design, and technology, these engineers were able to bridge the gap of software and hardware existences that is widely used and practiced today.

The constant production keeps hardwares up to date so it may function with modern softwares available.
Impact on Society

- Innovations in computer hardwares

- Improved communication technologies

- Gradual decrease in hardware energy cost

Information Technology
Concept
- By utilizing software, Information Technology revolves around the creation of Webs, Apps, Operating Systems, Networks, and even Cybersecurity.

- It is extremely crucial nowadays as the modern technology depends on the uses of IT and other computing professions
Impact on Society

- Improved digital securities

- Communication throughout the globe

- Digitalization of day to day tasks

Information Systems
Concept
- With other computing professions on the rise, Information System was used for mostly business and keeping accounts.

- Now used for a wider and versatile purpose, it manages processes, stores data for companies worldwide essential for keeping a company running.
Impact on Society

- Improve business processes and marketing

- Realtime analysis and breakdown of data

- Reduced redundant operations for efficiency

Software Engineering
Concept
- Just like Information Systems, this computing profession was on the rise with goal to develop up to date softwares through the use of theories, applications, and such.

- A trend in computing professions, SE handles development and optimizations for software to function properly or to what is its purpose.
Impact on Society

- User friendly softwares for easier use

- Innovate apps for different audiences

- Secure and reliable softwares for businesses and individuals
